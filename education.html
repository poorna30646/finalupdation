Protect students, teachers, and institutions from fake education portals

About This Extension

The Education Phishing Detection Extension is designed to protect students, faculty, and educational institutions from phishing attacks such as fake university portals, scholarship scams, online exam fraud, and learning platform impersonation.

Supported Browsers: Chrome, Edge

Installation Instructions

Step 1: Download the extension ZIP file.
Step 2: Extract the ZIP file.
Step 3: Open chrome://extensions.
Step 4: Enable Developer Mode.
Step 5: Click Load Unpacked and select the folder.

Setup & Usage Process

  • Extension activates automatically after installation.
  • Visit education-related websites (LMS, portals, exam sites).
  • Real-time scanning detects phishing or fake portals.
  • Instant alerts warn users before data is entered.
  • Popup explains risk level and safety guidance.
