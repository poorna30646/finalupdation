Protecting Users from Phishing & Malicious Websites

Secure Web Extensions is a cybersecurity project that provides browser-based protection against phishing attacks across business, education, banking, and cryptocurrency platforms.

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, bank details, or crypto wallet keys by pretending to be trusted emails, messages, or websites.

Why is Phishing Dangerous?

Phishing attacks can cause financial loss, identity theft, data breaches, and unauthorized access to personal or organizational accounts. A single mistake can compromise user security.

Common Types of Phishing

Email phishing, fake websites, SMS scams, banking fraud, education portal impersonation, and crypto wallet scams are among the most common phishing techniques today.

Email Phishing Detection

Detects fraudulent emails and phishing messages that attempt to steal login credentials and personal information.

Website Phishing Detection

Identifies fake and cloned websites such as banking, education, and business portals in real time.

SMS Phishing Detection

Protects users from malicious SMS links and scam messages used in phishing attacks.

Why This Project Matters

Phishing attacks are one of the most common cyber threats today. This project helps users stay safe online by increasing awareness and providing real-time protection against scams and fraud.
