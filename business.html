About This Extension

The Business Phishing Detection Extension helps organizations identify fraudulent business websites, fake login pages, and scam portals. It analyzes URLs, page content, and behavior patterns to warn users before they enter sensitive business credentials.

Supported Browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge

Installation Instructions

Step 1: Download the extension ZIP file from the button above.
Step 2: Extract the ZIP file to any folder on your system.
Step 3: Open your browser and go to:
chrome://extensions
Step 4: Enable Developer Mode (top-right corner).
Step 5: Click Load Unpacked and select the extracted folder.

Setup & Usage Process

  • Once installed, the extension icon will appear in your browser toolbar.
  • Open any business-related website (banking, CRM, company login pages).
  • The extension automatically scans the website in real-time.
  • If a phishing threat is detected, a warning alert is shown immediately.
  • You can view risk level, threat reason, and safety tips in the extension popup.

This extension is designed to work silently in the background and does not affect normal browsing speed.
